Course Reflection

Building modern websites means going beyond the basics of CSS, and Jonas Schmedtmann's Advanced CSS and Sass course on Udemy helped me do exactly that. This particular page reflects what I learned about Sass and CSS Grid throughout the course. One thing that really clicked for me was Jonas's approach to teaching Grid. He doesn't dive straight into complex layouts—instead, he starts with simple red boxes that make the whole system less intimidating. As I worked through the material, I truly gained an understanding of Grid while discovering some Sass techniques I hadn't used before. What really changed my perspective was learning that there are several solutions to reach the same goal. Seeing these different approaches has inspired me to be more creative with my methods in future projects and to tackle more ambitious designs.
